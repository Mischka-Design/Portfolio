Project information

Opportunity

I was assigned the task of helping an Italian client of ours turn his company, Italia Tourismus into its own unique brand, to bring extra value to their direct channel and help travellers understand the real advantage of booking directly online. I was also handed the opportunity of creating a logo, although I have zero experience in graphic design, I saw this as a fun challenge to test my range in the design field. I thought it would be perfect to create something that would complement Italia Tourismusâ€™ personality, hence, the Italian Flag. Its colours can be interpreted in two different ways. One is that green symbolizes hope, while white represents faith and red signifies charity. Another interpretation pronounces green to be the symbol of the Italian landscape, white as the snow capping the mountains of the Alps and red symbolizing the bloodshed that brought about the independence of Italy as a nation. All these symbols embody the presence and nationalism among the Italian people. It was my goal to carry over the message to the users by using vibrant images of Italian landscapes and providing a seamless exploratory experience that is pleasing to the eyes.

Approach

I worked on this project as a UX/UI Designer for Go4IT Africa, I was part of a small team alongside Brandon Clack. I was responsible for the research, user-experience, visual design and front-end development for Italia Tourismus. Instead of wireframing, I opted to sketch my designs on paper. I used paper prototyping techniques to bring the designs to life and evaluate them with our users. This helped me work more rapidly and led me to consider more ideas. Sketching many concepts helped me form a broader view of the Company, earlier ensuring a more cohesive design. I worked hard alongside Brandon (our inventory system expert and digital team lead) to bring the design to life. Communicating face-to-face and discussing constraints and possibilities was an effective way of solving the User experience and interface design. We were worked collaboratively, tested constantly and iterated progressively.

Solution

Italia Tourismus is live and growing to a very successful company in Italy. Offering an interactive map on their website creating a fun way to navigate to the desired destination and offers a seamless book and pay experience.
